lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagne is a timeless Italian comfort dish that's ideal for cozy family meals or impressing guests. This homemade version layers silky pasta sheets with a hearty meat sauce made from ground beef, tomatoes, and fragrant herbs, topped with creamy béchamel and bubbling mozzarella cheese. It's rich, satisfying, and full of flavor in every bite!

What sets this lasagne apart is the perfect harmony of textures—the tender noodles, savory ragu, and gooey cheese melt together in the oven to create a golden, crispy top. Prep it ahead and bake when ready; it even freezes beautifully for busy nights. Pair it with a crisp salad and crusty bread for the ultimate feast!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne sheets (no-boil or fresh)
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 2 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 200ml (¾ cup) red wine (optional)
  • 250ml (1 cup) beef stock
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking
  • 50g (3½ tbsp) butter
  • 50g (⅓ cup) all-purpose flour
  • 750ml (3 cups) whole milk
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • 300g (10 oz) mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 100g (1 cup) Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery. Cook for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Add the ground beef, breaking it up with a spoon, and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes. Pour in the red wine (if using) and let it simmer until mostly evaporated.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef stock, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low and let the sauce cook uncovered for 45-60 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
  5. While the sauce simmers, make the béchamel: Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook for 2 minutes. Gradually whisk in the milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Cook until thickened (coats the back of a spoon), about 8-10 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Set aside.
  6. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  7. Assemble the lasagne in a large baking dish (9x13 inches): Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Add a layer of lasagne sheets. Spread meat sauce, then béchamel, and sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan. Repeat layers (usually 3-4 layers total), finishing with béchamel and a generous topping of cheeses.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  9. Let the lasagne rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
