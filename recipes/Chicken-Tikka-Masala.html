Chicken Tikka Masala Recipe

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved curry featuring tender marinated chicken pieces in a vibrant, spiced tomato-cream sauce. Fragrant with garam masala, ginger, and garlic, it's mildly spicy, creamy, and utterly addictive.

The chicken is first marinated in yogurt and spices, then grilled or broiled for smoky flavor before simmering in the luxurious sauce. Serve over basmati rice with naan bread—it's restaurant-quality comfort food made at home!

Ingredients

  • 800g (1.75 lb) boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 200g (¾ cup) plain yogurt (for marinade)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 400g (14 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 200ml (¾ cup) heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Oil or butter for cooking
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, half the spices, garlic, and ginger. Add chicken, coat well, and marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight).
  2. Preheat broiler or grill. Thread chicken onto skewers and cook until charred and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes.
  3. In a large pan, heat oil/butter over medium heat. Sauté onion until golden.
  4. Add remaining garlic, ginger, and spices; cook for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked chicken and cream. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until sauce thickens.
  7. Season with salt, garnish with cilantro, and serve hot with rice or naan.
